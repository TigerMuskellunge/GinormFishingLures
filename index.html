WHAT DO WE SELL?

We are a based in Layton Utah, and we sell massive swimbaits, crankbaits, and spoons you can use to catch huge fish like Tiger Muskie, Trout, and Bass.

WHAT IS OUR MISSION?

Our mission at Ginorm Fishing Lures is to make Muskie fishermens lives easier by taking concepts already made, and improving them. We also make it easier for Utah fishermen to get really big lures with more hooks, so it is easier to hook the fish, and more realistic damage when the fish bite the lure like bleeding.
