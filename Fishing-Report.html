East Canyon

We went up on May 13, and caught 25 14-17 inch Rainbows. All of the fish were caught trolling pop-gear and a worm. We tried some casting in the morning, and had a few bit and hook ups, but we never landed any fish, and the bit was very slow casting. All in all we would say the fishing is good to hot if you troll the proper stuff.
